The 5-Day Schedule

One hour a day.
Permanent workflow change.

Sessions run over lunch — flexible to your team's schedule. Each day builds on the last.

D1
Day One
The Harness
How your AI workspace is structured — files, skills, and the wiki that gets smarter as you work. Every team member sets up their personal profile and builds their first real workflow.
Homework: Build your personal morning brief
D2
Day Two
Repeat Workflows
Automate the tasks you do every day — morning briefs, competitor research, news pulls — so your AI workspace is already working before you sit down.
Homework: Set up one recurring automated research task
D3
Day Three
Decks & Presentations
Generate and update presentations from raw notes, with your brand baked in. Optionally generate slide images locally — no subscriptions, no uploads.
Homework: Rebuild or update a real deck you own
D4
Day Four
PRDs & Docs
Turn voice memos and brain dumps into structured PRDs, meeting summaries, stakeholder updates, and discovery docs. Messy input is fine — that's the point.
Homework: Voice memo a feature idea → generate a full PRD
D5
Day Five
Proof of Concept
Concept to clickable prototype in under an hour. No design handoffs, no code. Generate a prototype brief and a prompt you can drop directly into v0.dev.
Homework: Build a PoC of anything
